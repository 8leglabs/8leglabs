Can You Prevent a Bad Moult?

Posted: May 26, 2025

While not every moult problem is preventable, most of them are. Bad moults often result from husbandry issues or avoidable stress — and the good news is, you can take action now to lower the risk.

Top 5 Ways to Prevent Moulting Issues

  • Hydration: Always have fresh water available. Dehydration is a major cause of failed moults.
  • Humidity: Know your species and maintain humidity appropriately. Too dry, and the spider may get stuck.
  • Peace and Quiet: Reduce vibrations, noise, and handling during pre-moult and after a moult.
  • Proper Setup: Avoid sharp décor, give room for stretching or hanging, and use safe substrate.
  • Internal Support: Good nutrition and internal hydration make a difference — especially during high-stress phases.
